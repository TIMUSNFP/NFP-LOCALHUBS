Understanding the Movement

What Are Local Hubs?

Think of Local Hubs as NetworkFP's regional chapters — where members within a geography come together to grow professionally and personally.

01

Learn Together

Access peer-driven learning sessions, case studies, and practitioner knowledge that elevates every member.

02

Build Community

Form genuine connections with fellow financial planners who understand your journey and share your values.

03

Collaborate

Exchange ideas, co-create solutions, and tackle challenges together with trusted peers in your region.

04

Grow Professionally

Sharpen your skills, expand your network, and accelerate your practice with community support behind you.

05

Develop Leaders

Step up, share your expertise, and shape the financial planning profession in your city and beyond.

The Case for Human Connection

As technology, AI, and digital interactions continue to grow, personal connection becomes even more valuable. The strongest learning often happens through peer-to-peer interaction.

Hub Membership

What Does It Mean to Be Part of a Hub?

Being part of a Local Hub is a commitment to yourself, your peers, and the profession.

01

Committed to Learning

Learn continuously with peers who face similar challenges and opportunities. Every session is an investment in your professional future.

02

Quarterly Meetups

Meet at least once every quarter with members in your region. Consistent, structured gatherings that build real relationships over time.

03

Buddy & Mentor Support

Be open to learning, mentoring, and being mentored as part of the community. Give what you have. Receive what you need.

Coverage

Where Can a Local Hub Be Started?

A Local Hub can be initiated in any city, region, or locality that has at least 5 active NetworkFP Members.

The goal is to ensure every financial planner in India, no matter where they are, has access to a supportive local community.

Mumbai Pune Bengaluru Chennai Hyderabad Delhi NCR Ahmedabad Kolkata Jaipur Kochi Chandigarh + Your City

These are examples only. Any city or locality with 5+ active members can start a hub.

Hub Leadership

Eligibility Criteria

01

Minimum 3 Years

At least 3 years of active NetworkFP Membership, demonstrating sustained commitment to the profession.

02

Office Space Ready

Has an office or venue capable of hosting at least 4 Personal Finance Professionals comfortably.

03

Believes in Community

Genuinely believes in the power of community and is committed to showing up consistently for peers.
